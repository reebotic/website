Firmware Engineer

San Jose, California

About the position

  • Job Number: 7001 1258
  • San Jose, California, United States
  • Posted: Jan 23, 2017
  • Weekly Hours: 40.00
    • In this role as the Embedded Firmware Engineer, you will implement common firmware interfaces, device drivers, communications protocols, diagnostics, factory calibration and test functionality across Apple's current and future embedded product line, including iPhone, iPad, iPod, and AppleTV. You must thrive in a fast-paced environment and enjoy solving problems, delivering and debugging high-quality software. Your contributions will make a pronounced difference to future embedded products.

    Description

    As an Embedded Firmware Engineer you will be responsible for creating and maintaining firmware running on all of Apple's embedded devices (iPhones, iPads, iPods, AppleTV). You will have responsibilities spanning all aspects of product bring-up and test: * Bring up new SoCs, chipsets, sensors, and products * Write, debug, and integrate new device drivers, protocols, and other firmware code across Apple's embedded product line * Interacting with Hardware, OS, Test, Operations and various other cross-functional teams throughout Apple * Review board schematics and data sheets * Utilize oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, and other test equipment to troubleshoot hardware/software issues * Enable test strategies for new components in upcoming products * Support product development builds locally and remotely * Experience in debugging low-level firmware and hardware is a plus.

    Key Qualifications

    • BS CS/EE/CE or relevant work experience (MS is a plus);
    • C experience (C++ is a plus);
    • Solid debugging ability;
    • Unix command line experience;
    • Development experience on Unix, Linux;
    • Effective communication skills;
