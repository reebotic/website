Hardware Systems Architect

San Jose, California

About the position

The UGV Team is seeking talented electrical engineering technical leadership in a System Architecture group. This is an individual contributor design engineering position. The ideal candidate has core competencies in several functional areas of responsibility and system design. The position requires the candidate to lead & drive system technical roadmaps. Experience is expected in analyzing key systems engineering architectural and implementation trade-offs; design of high performance, cost-effective, high quality products. The position requires strong communication, persuasion, and negotiation skills.

Key Qualifications

  • MS/PhD EE/CS w/5 years overall experience needed developing embedded system and robotics
  • Must have hands-on experience in prototype bring-up and debugging, functional verification, and manufacturing support
  • Must have strong experience in ROS.
  • Must have strong understanding in network programming
  • Strong programming skill in C++ w/ in depth understanding in OOD/

Description

Propose, challenge and influence x-functional roadmaps work with the UGV team on HW/SW robotics technologies. Keep current with technology trends & vendor roadmaps on the robotics sensors, technologies and architectus. Guide early system layout studies Work with Mechanical Engineering and Industrial Design. Predict & prepare for future product system integration & interconnect needs.
