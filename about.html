Our Story

By Reebotic
Date 3 Jan 2019

Reebotic is founded by a group of veteran Silicon Valley roboticists. We believe that in the near future, robots will become important in every facet of our daily lives, going beyond just vacuuming the floor and powering factories. We provide AI-powered robotic services and solutions to a broad range of industries and applications. Our high-performance, all-weather and all-terrain robot platforms are built on over 12 years experiences making robots for law enforcement, firefighters, and other public safety personnel who perform some of the toughest missions in the world. And our team has decades of experiences in high-quality products manufacturing. Our goal is to build robots that not only can save lives, but can also make this world a better place to live!
