Production Operations Engineer

San Jose, California

Responsibilities

  • Support and resolve issues with contract manufacturers, suppliers, and logistic agents to meet production’s lead time, quality, and cost goals.
  • Manage production resources and constraints to optimize company’s financial performance (profit, revenue, cash flow, and etc.)
  • Develop, maintain, and audit data systems to track production’s key performance metrics.
  • Utilize internal and external resources to continuously improve production’s key performance metrics.
  • Design and build flexible and robust procurement, production, and service systems suitable for high complexity, low/high-mix, and low/high-volume production.
  • Support potential US and worldwide market partners and customers.
