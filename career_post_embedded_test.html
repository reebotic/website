Embedded Test Engineer

San Jose, California

About the position

Some people think inside the box, some people think outside the box. If you have no idea what a box is, this position might be for you. We are looking for an Embedded Test Engineer who is passionate about testing. You will get your hands dirty designing, coding, maintaining, and extending test frameworks across a range of different projects and robot applications. You will code tests side-by-side with developers, anticipate test needs and proactively identify areas of improvement and new tools to incorporate into the testing and automation process. You will own the processes and tools and will advocate adherence to best practices. Using your hardware and software experience you will automate wherever possible using enterprise-level tools. Anything done manually is a candidate for automation.

Key Qualifications

  • 5+ years testing embedded systems and working in C/C++.
  • 3+ years working from requirements, architecture and high-level design to develop automated tests.
  • Work independently with minimal oversight in a highly collaborative environment.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills are a must.
  • Self-motivated with excellent time management skills and entrepreneurial spirit.
